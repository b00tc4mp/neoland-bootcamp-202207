Golf Saturday with friends

Sant Cugat del Vallès, 28 Mar 2022

9am tee time...

had planned to warm up and throw some balls before doing the first shot of the day, but as always I don’t calculate the time well and in the end I arrive very just in time. Two swings before the first shot. My back crunches. It is very important to start the first 9 holes with a good result so as not to lose motivation. Then everything is easier. It's already 11.30 and the spring sun is warming us up in the wooded hole 12. I'm 10 on and there's still half a game left, but we're having a great time, I love it

Finding new spots

Sant Cugat del Vallès, mar 30, 2022

7 morning, Himmafushi, Maldives...

We get up a little lazy. We eat 4 mini bananas from our friend Bito's garden. We wax the tables, put on sunscreen all over our bodies and take the bikes to get to the spot. We are alone, all the waves for us. We've been very lucky to be able to travel in the midst of a pandemic. My buddies and I still don't believe what we have in front of us: We are in November, on a paradise island in the Pacific surfing alone in one of the most famous spots in the world. It looks like a dream.
